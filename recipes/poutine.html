Poutine

back to Recipes

Source: Real Poutine - Allrecipes

Description

A famous Canadian delicacy consisting of fries and cheese pieces topped with gravy. Its origins date back to the 1950s in the province of Quebec.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart vegetable oil
  • 1 can beef gravy
  • 5 medium potatoes cut into fries
  • 2 cups cheese curds

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a deep fryer or heavy skillet to 365Â°F. Warm the gravy.
  2. Put the fries into the oil and cook for 8 to 10 minutes. Remove and place the fries on a power towel-lined plate for draining.
  3. Place the fries on a serving platter and sprinkle the cheese curds.
  4. Ladle the warmed gravy over the fries and serve.
