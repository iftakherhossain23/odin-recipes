Shawarma

back to Recipes

Source: Chicken Shawarma - Allrecipes

Description

A renowned Middle Eastern dish consisting of pieces of meat inside a pita bread wrap. Additional toppings include vegetables, such as radish, lettuce, and onions, and sauces, such as tahini and hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup malt vinegar
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon mixed spice
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground cardamom
  • 8 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • ½ cup tahini
  • ½ teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 4 medium tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup sliced onion
  • 4 cups shredded lettuce
  • 8 pita bread rounds

Steps

  1. Mix the vinegar, ¼ cup plain yogurt, vegetable oil, mixed spice, cardamom, salt, and pepper in a baking dish. Add the chicken thighs and turn to coat. Cover the dish and marinate in a refrigerator for at least 4 hours.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Mix the tahini, ¼ cup plain yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, and parsley in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Cover the bowl and refrigerate.
  3. Cover the chicken and bake it in the marinade for 30 minutes (turn it once). Uncover it and cook for 5 to 10 minutes. Remove and cut into slices.
  4. Put the chicken, tomato, onion, and lettuce onto pita bread. Roll it up and top it with tahini sauce.
